# E-Commerce Sales Analysis Findings

Dataset Size:
50,000 transactions
100 unique customers

Key Findings:

• Total Revenue: R25,157,989.65

• Average Transaction Value:
R503.16

• Average Customer Spend:
R251,579.90

• Most Purchased Category:
Toys (6,392 purchases)

• Highest Revenue Category:
Sports (R3,195,335.90)

• Most Preferred Payment Method:
UPI (8,477 transactions)

• Highest Revenue Market:
France (R2,545,739.19)

Business Recommendations:

1. Increase investment in Sports products because they generate the highest revenue.

2. Maintain adequate inventory for Toys because of high customer demand.

3. Optimize UPI payment processing because it is the preferred payment method.

4. Develop loyalty programs for high-value customers.

5. Investigate why France performs exceptionally well and replicate successful strategies in other markets.
