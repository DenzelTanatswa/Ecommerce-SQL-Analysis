\# E-Commerce Sales Analysis Using SQL



\## Project Overview



This project analyzes 50,000 e-commerce transactions using PostgreSQL to identify customer purchasing patterns, product performance, payment preferences, and revenue trends.



\## Tools Used



\- PostgreSQL

\- SQL

\- pgAdmin

\- CSV Dataset

\- GitHub



\## Business Questions Answered



1\. How many transactions were processed?

2\. What was the total revenue generated?

3\. What is the average transaction value?

4\. Which countries generate the most revenue?

5\. Which product categories perform best?

6\. Which payment methods are most popular?

7\. Who are the highest-value customers?



\## Key Findings



\- Total Revenue: R25,157,989.65

\- Average Transaction Value: R503.16

\- Average Customer Spend: R251,579.90

\- Most Purchased Category: Toys

\- Highest Revenue Category: Sports

\- Most Preferred Payment Method: UPI

\- Highest Revenue Market: France



\## Business Recommendations



\- Increase investment in Sports products.

\- Maintain inventory for Toys.

\- Optimize UPI payment processing.

\- Create loyalty programs for high-value customers.

\- Investigate high-performing markets such as France.
